Dispensing
1. User scans RFID tag
2. Dispenser fetches medication data. Gives feedback if not right time, not right machine, etc.
3. Fetch compartment locations.
4. Look at location 1. Start motor.
5. When pill detected, kill motor.
6. Weigh pills.
7. Repeat step 4 until we have required number of pills.
8. Dispense pills if weight correct.
